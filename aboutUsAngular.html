About Persiadog

the Biggest Pet Store in Shiraz

Persiadog is a locally owned and operated dogs, dog food and supply store. Our mission is to provide dog parents with everything that they need to ensure their dog lives a long and happy life. We love to talk about dogs of all types and we are even more excited when you bring your furry family member into the store to meet us! We are constantly researching and learning about new products that can make our pets lives better. we started our business at School of Veterinary Science
